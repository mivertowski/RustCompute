\section{Discussion}
\label{sec:discussion}

Stub: Limits of the model (we don't model warp divergence
explicitly; cooperative-group failure semantics under hardware
fault remain heuristic). Avenues: extending to multi-vendor
backends (AMD HIP, Intel oneAPI), formalizing migration semantics
across devices (only model-checked, not analytically proved),
NSAI integration as the motivating workload. Open question: can
we model-check at scales relevant to production GPU workloads,
or do we need symbolic abstraction techniques?
